    Ultima 6 Randomizer Information

    The Ultima 6 SNES Randomizer allows you to randomize a number of different elements to Ultima 6, giving you a endless amount of unqiue replays of the game.

    Overview
    • The Item Randomizer allows you to randomly shuffle items around in the world for when you want to enjoy an item hunt and more exploration.
    • The Enemy Randomizer allows you to shuffle enemies and their properties creating new combat experiences and challenges.
    Item Randomization Details
    The Item Randomizer will randomly shuffle all selected items between one another. If Main Items and Overworld Chests are selected, then Runes and Moonstones could be placed in any overworld chest!

    The Item Randomizer will attempt to prevent any placement of items which would result in them being unreachable. If there are multiple items in a chest, the randomizer will choose to randomize only one item per chest.

    You may choose to add the Moon Orb and/or Spellbook to the item randomizer pool, which will result in you starting with a random item from the item pool in their spot in your inventory.

    Enemy Randomization Details
    The Enemy Randomizer allows you to randomize enemy and creature spawns.

    If Randomly Mix is NOT selected, then creatures will only randomize with their own type, so monsters can only appear where other monsters could appear.

    If Randomly Mix IS selected then all selected creature types are mixed together. Monsters can now appear where anything else selected could appear!

    You can also Randomize Aggression, which will shuffle the aggression of all spawns around. Sheep or guards in town could now attack you!
