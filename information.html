The Ultima 6 SNES Randomizer allows you to randomize a number of different elements to Ultima 6, giving you a endless amount of unqiue replays of the game.

Overview
  • The Item Randomizer allows you to randomly shuffle items around in the world for when you want to enjoy an item hunt.
  • The Enemy Randomizer allows you to shuffle enemies and their properties creating new combat experiences.
Item Randomization Details
    The Item Randomizer will randomly shuffle all selected items between one another. It will attempt to prevent any placement of items which would result in them being unreachable. If there are multiple items in a chest, the randomizer will choose to randomize only one item per chest. You may choose to add the Moon Orb and/or Spellbook to the item randomizer pool, which will result in you starting with a random item from the item pool in their spot in your inventory.
