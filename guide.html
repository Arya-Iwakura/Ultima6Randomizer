by squibbons

Quickstart Guide

New to Ultima 6 for the SNES, need a refresher, or want tips that may help you along your randomized journey? Then this guide is for you! This is a quickstart guide meant to give you the bare minimum knowledge needed to help you along your way. The View Information panel on the randomizer page also contains extra information about the item locations. For more in depth information, check out any available strategy guides and of course maps you can find online!

Goal

The ultimate goal of Ultima 6 Randomizer is to beat the game. To do this at a minimum you will need to:
  • Collect the 8 Moonstones
  • Collect the Vortex Cube and insert the 8 Moonstones into it
  • Craft the Gargoyle Lens from the Broken Lens
  • Craft the Human Lens from a Glass Sword and the Gargoyle Lens
  • Craft the Balloon using the Balloon Plans
  • Visit the 3 Gargoyle Shrines (Diligence, Control, and Passion)
  • Have at least 80 Karma
  • Have a Spellbook with the spell Dispel and visit the Shrine of Singularity via Balloon
  • Visit the Shrine of the Avatar to finish the game
Depending on how the items are randomized you are very likely to need to do quite a bit more. Other important items that you will likely need are:
  • The 8 Runes which are used to unlock the 8 Shrines(Each has an item)
  • The Moon Orb is used to quickly teleport around the world
  • The Spell Book allows you to cast spells
  • The Spell - Unlock allows you to unlock magically locked doors and chests
  • The Spell - Dispel Field allows you to remove magical fields that block your path
  • Sherry the Mouse who will give you access to an item in the Jhelom Tavern
  • A Shovel which will allow you to dig an entrance into Pirate Cave
  • Powder Kegs which can be used to destroy non-magically locked doors

Getting Started Tips

  • Talk to Lord British to get the item Key B to exit the castle.
  • Give some Cheese to Sherry the Mouse. She is normally found along the outer rooms of the castle.(She is needed to get an item)
  • Open the 3 Chests to get three randomized items along with anything else the chests have.
  • If Hints are Enabled get the Book from the chest in the small room and check Lord British's Locked room.
  • Take anything you want from the castle as everything is free and nothing is considered stolen.
  • Use the Key to exit the castle through the front gate.
  • Talk to Ariana (child in Music Hall south-west of Castle gate) and get the chests in Britain.
  • If you find the Rune of Compassion you can follow the road east out of town to get an item from the Shrine.
  • From here visit any cities you can reach. If playing without the Moon Orb, Paws and Skara Brae are good early places to visit.

Other Useful Tips

  • There are many hidden passages in the game. Look for the wall pattern to be different. There is one in Lord British's closet!
  • Selling items to Gwenneth in Britain is generally a good way to make money.
  • Invisibility Rings are incredibly useful but have a chance of breaking every step you take while wearing one.
  • Using Powder Kegs to destroy doors is a sometimes necessary way to enter locked doors, so always carry a few.
  • Buying a Skiff is useful to reach islands.
  • NEVER leave your Skiff or Balloon behind!!! They will be deleted and you will need to buy or craft a new one!

The 8 Virtues

Ultima is a game about virtues. You play as the Avatar, the paragon of virtue. There are 8 virtues in the game. Each virtue has a City, a Shrine, and a Rune that unlocks the Shrine. Shrines have items and allow you to level up and gain karma.
  • Compassion is the city of Britain.
    • The Rune of Compassion has a Heart symbol
    • The Moon Orb teleport is 2 up
  • Honesty is the city of Moonglow.
    • The Rune of Moonglow has a Hand symbol
    • The Moon Orb teleport is 2 up and 2 left
  • Honor is the city of Trinsic.
    • The Rune of Honor has a Chalice symbol
    • The Moon Orb teleport is 2 down
  • Humility is the city of New Maginica.
    • The Rune of Humilty has a Crook symbol
    • The Moon Orb teleport is 2 left
  • Justice is the city of Yew.
    • The Rune of Justice has a Scales symbol
    • The Moon Orb teleport is 2 right
  • Sacrifice is the city of Minoc.
    • The Rune of Sacrifice has a Drop symbol
    • The Moon Orb teleport is 2 down and 2 right
  • Spirituality is the city of Skara Brae.
    • The Rune of Spirituality has an Ankh symbol
    • The Moon Orb teleport is 2 down and 2 left
  • Valor is the city of Jhelom.
    • The Rune of Valor has a Sword symbol
    • The Moon Orb teleport is 2 up and 2 right

The Moon Orb

The Moon Orb (otherwise known as the Orb of the Moons) lets you quickly teleport to 22 locations. To use it, simply throw it on the ground near you. You can throw it up to two tiles away, and where you throw it determines where you will teleport. Experiment with it and you will quickly learn how it works.

You can use it to teleport to:
  • The 8 Virtue Cities(2 tiles out along the cardinal and ordinal directions with you at the center)
  • The 8 Shrines(one tile clockwise from each city)
  • 3 of the Gargoyle Shrines (Down, Up Left, and Up Right)
  • The Gargoyle City (Down Left)
  • The Shrine of the Avatar (Down Right)
  • Lord British (Up)

Spells and Reagents

To cast spells you must have a Spellbook equipped and know the spell. The two require spells are Unlock which can normally be purchased in Skara Brae and Dispel Field which can normally be purchased in Moonglow. You will likely often cast Light in caves and at night, and Dispel Magic to cure poisons. Help is a free spell that will teleport you to Lord British.

Spells cost reagents to use. The following reagents are needed for the most used spells:
  • Dispel Field requires Black Pearl and Sulphurous Ash
  • Dispel Magic requires Garlic and Ginseng
  • Heal requires Ginseng and Spidersilk
  • Light requires Sulphurous Ash
  • Unlock requires Blood Moss and Sulphurous Ash

Karma

You will need to manage your Karma in the game, represented in your inventory screen as KR. Karma affects things such as what quests you can do, how NPCs respond to you, the prices of items in shops, and how much XP you keep on death.

Karma is lost for doing bad actions, such as lying to children, stealing, and attacking non-violent characters.

Karma is gained when doing Quests and mainly from Meditiating at Shrines. Meditating is very important as it also can gain you levels and stats. To beat the game you will need at least 80 karma. To complete the Gargoyle Lens, you will need at least 75 karma.

No Main Items are treated as being stolen. All items in Shrines and Underground are also never treated as being stolen.

Important Quest Solutions

  • Visit Ariana the child usually found in the Britain Music Hall. She has an item when asked about the Rune.
    • To get her parents permission and to not lose karma on the quest, visit her mother in the tavern on the east side of town.
    • You can lie to the child and simply take the item for a karma loss.
  • Visit Antonio the mayor in New Magincia. He has an item when asked about the Rune.
    • Antonio will tell you to talk to a series of characters in the town.
    • William is the farmer and can often be found in the north in the fields.
    • Aurendir is the sheep herder who spends time at the moongate.
    • Conor is a fisherman and can often be found on the docks to the south.
    • Charlotte is the weaver in town and spends much of her time in the shop.
    • Dunbar is the cook and is often in the tavern.
  • Visit Selganor the Musician Guild leader in Minoc. He has an item when asked about the Rune.
    • For this you will also need Yew Log bought from Big Ben hidden away in the forest near Yew.
    • Then cut the log into Boards at the lumbermill in Minoc.
    • You may need to talk to Selganor first before you can continue. He is usually in the Music Hall in Minoc.
    • Then craft the Panpipes from the board from Julia in Minoc. She is usually in the north east shop.
    • Finally learn the song Stones from Gwenno who wanders around the streets of Minoc.
  • Once you have the Balloon Plans you will need to do the following to craft the Balloon.
    • Talk to Thindle in Paws and then have Arbeth craft you Silk Thread. This requires 40 Spidersilk and 20 gold.
    • Then talk to Charlotte in New Maginca to obtain Silk Cloth. This requires 10 gold.
    • Then return to Paws and talk to Marissa to get the Silk Bag. This requires 75 gold.
    • Get the Basket from Michelle in Minoc. This requires 300 gold.
    • Obtain a Cauldron(Empty Pot) and some Rope. These can be bought, found, or stolen. Rope is bought in Paws at Rope Maker; Cauldron in Britain Guild Shop.
    • When you have all the materials and the Balloon Plans, simply use the Balloon Plans and the Balloon will appear at your feet.

Beating the Game

  • Talk to Beh Lem and Captain John in the SE Gargoyle lands and Hythloth to learn Gargish Language.
  • Talk to the gargoyle Valkadesh in the SE of the Gargoyle Lands. Then surrender to the gargoyle Draxinusom near the gargoyle museum. This requires 75 karma.
  • Talk to the gargoyle Naxatilor near the gargoyle museum then get the Broken Lens repaired by the Gargoyle Lenscrafter in the east.
  • Visit the 3 Gargoyle Shrines (Diligence, Control, and Passion) and talk to the statues inside.
  • Visit the Shrine of Singularity via Balloon in the Gargoyle lands and talk to the large stone triangle. This requires 80 karma.
  • Get the Human Lens by visting the lenscrafter north of Moonglow. Requires the Broken Lens and a Glass Sword.
  • Obtain the 8 Moonstones and insert them into the Vortex Cube.
  • Finally, visit the Shrine of the Avatar and place the two Lens to the sides of the pedestal and the Vortex Cube on the pedestal to beat the game!

Dungeon and Puzzle Solutions

Britain Sewers

The Britain Sewers connect the city of Britain, Castle Britannia, and the town of Buccaneer's Den.
  • From Castle Britannia, use the ladder in the potion room, then use KeyB and follow the west passage and down the ladder.
  • In the cave, go east over the bridge then immediately south and around the wall to the left and go down the ladder.
  • Follow the wall north and west to find a trapped passage. There are three chests behind this area.

Buccaneer's Cave

Buccaneer's Cave connects the city of Britain, Castle Britannia, and the town of Buccaneer's Den.
  • From Buccaneer's Den, enter the cave behind Budo's Secret House.
  • Go west and north and down the ladder then go west to find the chest on the north wall in the open room.

Dungeon Destard

The dungeon Destard is extremely deadly and full of lava.
  • From the entrance head north then east and follow the wall south east.
  • At the large bones head west and south and go down the ladder in the next set of large bones.
  • On Floor 2 head north then follow the wall east and south. Enter the hole just to the west of the large bones.
  • On Floor 3 head west then south and west and go up the ladder back to Floor 2.
  • On Floor 2 again head to the west wall and find the passage that leads to the treasure area and the chest.

Dungeon Wrong / Covetous

The dungeon Wrong / Covetous has a pretty simple solution.
  • From the center of the dungeon, enter the room just to the up and left and go down the ladder. Will require a magic unlock cast.
  • Go directly up and flip only the switch at around 10 to 11 o'clock. Requires a magic unlock cast.
  • Go to the far left and enter the upper room and flip the left switch.
  • Go to the far right and flip the switch then return to the far left to go down the ladder.
  • Go east and down the hall then north through the secret walls in the room.

Gargoyle Shrine of Control

The Gargoyle Shrine of Control has a pretty big switch puzzle. Order of flipping the switches does not matter.
  • Flip all switches on the left side of the room except the bottom left corner.
  • Flip the two switches to the right of the bottom left corner switch.
  • Don't forget the switch on the left wall!
  • After flipping them all follow the hall north to the statue.

Gargoyle Shrine of Dilligence

The Gargoyle Shrine of Dilligence has a maze of rooms with a simple solution.
  • From the entrance head south two rooms then east one. There is a secret wall in the north east of this room.
  • Enter the secret room and go down the ladder then follow the hall west to find the statue.

Moonglow Catacombs

The Tomb of Beyvin is located in the Moonglow Catacombs. There are two entrances to the catacombs. To enter the tomb you will need to do the questline for the key or bring a Powder Keg.
  • Entrance One: In the tavern in south east Moonglow. There is a secret wall that leads to a ladder in the storage rooms.
  • Floor 1 from Entrance One: From this entrance go west over the poison to find the ladder at the far wall.
  • Entrance Two: In Xiao's Magic Shop north west of Moonglow there is a ladder in the closet.
  • Floor 1 from Entrance Two: From this entrance go west and continue along the long winding path until you find the ladder.
  • On Floor 2 head south east and enter the center tomb. With either the key or Powder Keg. Loot the tomb for the item.

Pirate Cave

The Pirate Cave is on a island south of New Maginica and requires a shovel to enter. The cave is quite long in the form of a multiple floor maze.
  • Dig on the patches of dirt found among the swamp covered island to find the hole and enter it.
  • From the entrance head north, then west, then south over the bridge.
  • At the end of the hall head north, then east to the hall end, north then east past the poison and south until the ladder.
  • On Floor 2 enter the maze in the south west. Up at first turn, left at second turn and then west and down the ladder.
  • On Floor 3 take the west passage, through the poison, then go north and east into a small room with a hole. Go down it.
  • On Floor 4 go east for a while until you reach a a dispel-able poison field. At the second field dispel it and go north.
  • Use a Powder Keg to enter the treasure house and gain access to the 5 chests here.

Stonegate

Stonegate is south of Minoc and requires a Powder Keg and the Dispel Field spell.
  • In the entrance area enter the secret door in the center wall and go down the ladder.
  • Flip the upper switch then return up the ladder.
  • Go down the upper right ladder, then down the upper middle ladder, then down the upper right ladder.
  • Go down the lower left ladder then go left through the door and use the lower wall to flip a switch.
  • Enter the room and use the secret upper wall, dispel the field, then explode the door with a Powder Keg.
  • Dispel the field in the next room and search the chest for an item.

Sutek's Castle

Sutek's Castle is on an island in the south and requires a Powder Keg and both the Dispel Field and Unlock spells. This castle has many secret passages.
  • Use a Powder Keg to enter the castle then go through the secret north wall passage.
  • Go left and in the room with the two doors, use the east wall to reveal a hidden central room.
  • Dispel the left fields and flip the left two switches and return to the left room.
  • Cast Unlock on the left door, enter the small room, then use the secret north wall door.
  • Enter the next small room then go east to the center small room and use the south secret wall door and go down the ladder.
  • In the upper north west room flip the upper right wall switch then exit this floor via the middle south room.
  • Go through the secret north wall then down the ladder in the right hallway.
  • Go right at the first choice and follow the path to the ladder and go down it.
  • Go left then up and follow the upper wall west until you reach a body. Search it for an item.
